Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes.
  3. Once garlic turns golden, add the drained spaghetti to the pan.
  4. Toss until spaghetti is well coated in the garlic oil.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and serve with Parmesan if desired.
