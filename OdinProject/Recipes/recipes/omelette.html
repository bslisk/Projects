Classic Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (e.g., cheddar or feta)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Saut√© onions and bell peppers until softened.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture, ensuring it spreads evenly.
  5. Once edges start to set, sprinkle cheese over half the omelette.
  6. Fold the other half over the cheese. Cook until fully set.
  7. Serve hot.
